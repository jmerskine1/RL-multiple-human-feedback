Human feedback for Pacman

Instructions


To rank actions for our RL Pacman agent:

  1. Look at the current state. Faded sprites show where Pacman and the ghost were in the last two steps.

  2. Click each arrow (or press each arrow key) to rank the four directions from best (1st click) to worst (4th click). Button colours update to reflect your ranking.

  3. Clicking an already-ranked button moves it to the end of your ranking.

  4. Once happy with your ranking, click 'Submit' or press SPACE. This advances to the next time step.

  5. When an episode finishes a button will appear to start a new episode.

  6. You can skip between time steps without submitting using 'Previous' and 'Next'.

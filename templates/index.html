Human feedback for Pacman

Instructions


To provide feedback to our RL pacman agent:

  1. Look at the current state. Decide which direction would be best for Pacman to move.

  2. Select that direction using the arrow buttons or your arrow keys. Faded sprites show where Pacman and the ghost were in the last two steps.

  3. Submit your feedback by clicking 'Submit' or pressing SPACE. This moves to the next time step.

  4. When an episode ends a button will appear to start a new one.

  5. You can skip between time steps using 'Previous' and 'Next' without submitting.
